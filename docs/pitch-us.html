01. Application Submission
Create an account and fill out our application form providing details about your company, team, product or service, market opportunity, and funding requirements. Attach essential documents such as your business plan, pitch deck, financial projections, and any other requested materials
02. initial Screening
Our team conducts an initial screening of all applications received assessing the alignment of your company with our investment criteria, including industry focus, stage of growth, and funding needs.
03. Due Diligence
If your application passes the initial screening, we initiate a detailed due diligence process. Our team performs a thorough examination of your company's financials, market potential, competitive landscape, intellectual property, team, and other relevant factors.
04. Investment Proposal
Based on the results of the due diligence process, we decide whether to move forward with an investment proposal. If we are interested, we present you with a term sheet outlining the proposed investment terms, including funding amount, equity stake, and any specific conditions or requirements.
05. Consultation Period
Once the term sheet is presented, A CONSULTATION PERIOD GETS UNDERWAY to finalize the investment terms and conditions. We work closely with you to reach a mutually beneficial agreement.
06. Closing the Deal
Once the CONSULTATION PERIOD HAS concluded and all parties are satisfied, the final investment agreements are signed. We transfer the agreed-upon funds to your company, completing the investment process.
07. Post-Investment Support
Our involvement does not end with the investment. we offer comprehensive post-investment support to the entrepreneurs and companies we invest in. Our dedicated team provides strategic guidance, operational expertise, valuable industry connections, and access to resources.
08. Follow up funding
The best performers across our portfolio have the opportunity to secure follow-on funding. We actively support companies that demonstrate exceptional potential and progress, providing them with the necessary resources to fuel their continued growth.
