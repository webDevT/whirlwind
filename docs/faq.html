Frequently asked
questions

What is Whirlwind Capital?
Whirlwind Capital is a global investment fund, focused on curating a diverse range of investment opportunities spanning multiple asset classes, industries, and regions.
In which jurisdictions is Whirlwind Capital regulated?
The operations of Whirlwind Capital currently falls under the regulatory oversight of the British Virgin Islands.
What asset classes can I invest In with Whirlwind Capital?
Whirlwind Capital specializes in alternative investments, which includes private equity, real estate, private credit and infrastructure.
What type of investment strategy does Whirlwind Capital offer?
At Whirlwind, we follow a dynamic approach to investment, focusing on deal-by-deal opportunities that span various stages and industries. Our strategy is rooted in identifying exceptional companies, both established and growth-oriented from around the globe.
What makes Whirlwind Capital unique?
At Whirlwind our strategy is unconstrained by asset class, industry, sector, or geography. Taking advantage of our global footprint, we provide co-investors with access to key international markets and deals across the globe.
How does Whirlwind Capital source its deals?
By partnering with us, investors can access a wide range of exclusive investment opportunities while benefiting from our expertise, network, and proven track record.
Can I redeem my money at any time?
Our far reaching network and wide ranging relationships allow us to source quality deal flow not readily accessible to individual investors.
What Is the minimum investment amount?
A minimum capital commitment of $250,000 is required to start investing with Whirlwind Capital. To learn more, please request an invite to join our exclusive investment platform and a member of our experienced investor team will review your request and assist you with the process.
Does Whirlwind Capital offer Shariah compliant investments?
Whirlwind offers Shariah-compliant investment opportunities in multiple asset classes which have been vetted and approved by certified Shariah scholars who sit on our Shariah advisory board. To know more about our Shariah compliant investments please reach out to us by emailing hello@whirlwindcapital.com
How can I start investing with Whirlwind?
Please request an invitation to join the Whirlwind Investment platform. Following your application a member of the Whirlwind team investor team will review your request and provide assistance throughout the onboarding process should you meet the qualifying criteria.

As a global investment fund, Whirlwind capital meticulously curates a diverse range of investment opportunities spanning multiple asset classes, industries, and regions. Our mission is to provide exclusive access to investment opportunities that consistently deliver attractive returns for our valued co-investors.
