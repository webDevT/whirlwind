INVESTMENT APPROACH

WE see the world differently

At Whirlwind, we follow a dynamic approach to investment, focusing on deal-by-deal opportunities that span various stages and industries.

Our strategy is rooted in identifying exceptional companies, both established and growth-oriented, from around the globe, and aligning our investments with their unique potential for success.

Flexible investment Size
We offer the flexibility to invest across a wide range of deal sizes, tailoring our investments to match the specific needs and growth trajectories of the companies we partner with. From early-stage ventures seeking seed or Series A funding to established companies requiring larger growth capital, we adapt our investment size accordingly.
Early-stage venture
We provide vital seed and early-stage funding to promising ventures, supporting their growth and development. Our investments in these companies typically ranges from $100,000 - $5 million, assisting them in achieving critical milestones and strategic initiatives.
Growth-Stage Companies
As companies mature and demonstrate significant growth potential, we offer larger investment sizes to support their expansion plans. Our investments in growth-stage companies typically range from $5 million - $25 million and are tailored to provide the necessary capital to fuel their scaling efforts, enter new markets, or drive innovation.
Targeted Allocation
While investment sizes may vary, we maintain a disciplined approach to capital allocation. Our investment team conducts thorough analysis and due diligence to assess each opportunity's risk-return profile, growth potential, and alignment with our overall portfolio strategy.
Strategic Partner
Our aim is to be a strategic partner, providing not just financial support but also guidance, network access, and operational expertise to drive value creation and deliver compelling risk-adjusted returns for our valued co-investors.
